Hi Everyone! I am the Vice president of the American Medical Students Association chapter at Hunter. This club represents my personal value of recognizing minorities in the medical field and providing medical care to the underserved community. Personally, I came from a third world country that was war torn, and I experienced the lack of medical innovation when I lived there. That is one of the reasons I have decided to take part in this club and spread acknowledgement about the struggles in health care, to many other pre-medical students. I like to make the pre-medical journey a healthy and prosperous environment, and my personal goal is to help anyone that is struggling realize that they are capable of achieving what they desire in the pre-medical journey.
